Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I import a route?

    Hootcycle supports routes with the .fit file format. We like using Ride with Gps for both finding and creating routes! If you find/build a route you like, export it as a .fit file, and save it to your phone. Then in the Hootcycle Routes menu, click 'Import' route.
  • How do I record a ride?

    Start a new ride by tapping the "Record" button on the main screen. The app will use your location and altitude to track your ride and save to a .gpx file.
  • How do I sync with Strava?

    Connect your Strava account in the app settings menu. All recorded rides will be automatically synced to your Strava account. If you have any issues please reach out to us directly for support.
