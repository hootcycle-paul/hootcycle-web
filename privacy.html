Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the HootCycle application.

While using our app, we may collect anonymous usage data to help improve our services. This data is collected by us and by third-party services, such as Mapbox. The usage data may include information about your device, application usage, and general location information.

The anonymous usage data we collect is used to enhance the user experience, improve our app, and analyze trends. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your anonymous data to outside parties.
